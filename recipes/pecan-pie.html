Pecan Pie with literally nothing but corn syrup in it basically!

How the hell do I make it???

First let's make a pie Crust!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 stick of butter frozen that's either grated chopped into pea sized pieces then re-frozen
  • ice water

Instructions

  1. Combine salt and flour
  2. Mix the diced butter into the mixture and coat all pieces are coated
  3. add 1 Tbl spoon of ice-water until crust just starts to hold shape, you want it to be slightly shaggy, it usually takes 10 or so. It just kind of depends.
  4. when mixed, but not over mixed, form into a hockey puck and cover in seran wrap to let rest in the fridge for 30 minutes or until properly chilled
  5. when chilled, flour work surface and rolling pin then roll into proper size for pan and lay into the pan
